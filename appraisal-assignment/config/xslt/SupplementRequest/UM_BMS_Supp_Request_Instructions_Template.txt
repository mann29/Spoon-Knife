Supplement Instructions for UltraMate Estimates

1.  Open eClaim Manager.
2.  Locate and open the Claim Folder in eClaim Manager.
3.  Launch UltraMate from within eClaim Manager.
4.  Update the estimate in UltraMate as appropriate.
5.  Calculate and Commit the estimate to return to eClaim Manager.
6.  Click the lightning bolt icon to send the estimate to the Insurance Company.
